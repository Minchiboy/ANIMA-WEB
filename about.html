About Anima

Anima is an experimental music label based in Rome, born from the desire to explore the emotional, atmospheric, and physical dimensions of sound. We operate in the space where ambient textures, electronic structures, and sonic abstraction converge,a place beyond genre, where music becomes presence, ritual, and landscape.

Founded by a collective of artists, curators, and designers, Anima is more than a label: it is a platform for sonic research and collaboration. We support emerging and established voices who approach music as a form of expression that is fluid, immersive, and deeply human.

Our work includes physical and digital releases, live performances, listening experiences, and interdisciplinary events. Whether through intimate sound installations or large-scale performances, we aim to create moments of disorientation, reflection, and connection.

At Anima, we believe that sound is not just something to be heard ‚Äì it is something to be inhabited.
We are here to amplify the unheard, give space to the subtle, and invite listeners into new territories of perception.

About Us

Anima is shaped by a small team of artists and collaborators who live between disciplines, sound, image, space, and experience. Each of us brings a different perspective, but we share a common goal: to challenge how music is created, shared, and felt.
